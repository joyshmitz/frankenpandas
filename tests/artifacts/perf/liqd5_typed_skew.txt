through numeric_moments (a typed single-pass Welford accumulation), but skew and
kurtosis collect their values through numeric_values, which materialized
col.values() into a Vec<Scalar> + per-element to_f64 before the moment passes.

collect present values straight from the f64 + validity slices, in index order.
